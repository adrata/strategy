## Proof Point Hierarchy

Use this order for maximum impact:

### 1. Specific Numbers
*"11.7 hours saved per week"* (concrete)

### 2. Comparative Stats
*"98.7% vs 85% industry average"* (better than alternatives)

### 3. Client Testimonials
*"Sarah said it gave her life back"* (emotional proof)

### 4. Case Studies
*"Heritage Title saved $18K"* (detailed proof)

### 5. Industry Trends
*"87% of title companies report..."* (you're not alone)

---

## The Credibility Stack

Build credibility in layers:

### Layer 1: Industry Understanding
*"Most title companies we talk to say..."*
(Shows you know their world)

### Layer 2: Specific Results
*"We helped a company just like yours save..."*
(Proof it works)

### Layer 3: Social Proof
*"47,000 successful signings..."*
(Scale and reliability)

### Layer 4: Authority
*"Trusted by 200+ title companies..."*
(Others believe in you)

### Layer 5: Guarantee
*"If you don't save time in the first 30 days..."*
(Risk reversal)
