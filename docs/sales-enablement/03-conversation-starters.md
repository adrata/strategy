## Referral Introductions

### When Someone Introduces You:
*"[Referrer] mentioned you're dealing with notary coordination challenges. They said the same thing before we helped them cut their coordination time by 90%. Mind if I show you what we did?"*

### When You're Asking for a Referral:
*"Who else do you know in title/escrow who's frustrated with notary scheduling? I'd love to help them the way we helped you."*

---

## Event/Networking Openers

### At Industry Events:
- *"What's the biggest operational challenge you're facing right now?"* (Listen for notary pain)
- *"How many closings are you doing per month? How are you handling the notary coordination?"*
- *"If you could automate one thing in your business, what would it be?"*

### The Follow-Up:
*"You mentioned [pain point] at the conference. I've been thinking about that - we just helped someone with the exact same issue. Got 10 minutes this week?"*

---

## Follow-Up Sequences

### After Initial Contact (No Response):
**Day 3:**
*"[Name] - following up on my note about notary coordination. Bad timing? Or not a priority right now?"*

**Day 7:**
*"Last one, I promise! Quick question: If I could show you how to save 10 hours/week on notary coordination, would that be worth 15 minutes?"*

### After Demo (Moving to Close):
**Day 1:**
*"Thanks for your time today! What was the most interesting part for you?"*

**Day 3:**
*"Been thinking about your comment on [pain point]. Here's how [similar client] solved that exact issue..."*

---

## LinkedIn/Social Approaches

### Connection Request Message:
*"Hi [Name] - I help title companies automate notary coordination. Saw you're at [Company] - would love to connect!"*

### After They Accept:
*"Thanks for connecting! Curious - how is [Company] handling notary scheduling these days? Most title companies we talk to say it's their #1 operational headache."*

### Content Engagement:
When they post about challenges:
*"This resonates! We just helped a title company solve this exact problem. Happy to share what worked if you're interested."*

---

## Phone Openers

### Cold Call Opening:
*"Hi [Name], this is [You] with Notary Everyday. Quick question - do you have 30 seconds? ... Great! When a notary cancels last minute, who's responsible for finding a replacement?"*

### Warm Call (After Email):
*"Hi [Name], following up on my email about notary coordination. Did you get a chance to see it? ... No worries! Let me ask you this: How much time does your team spend coordinating notaries each week?"*

---

## The Universal Opener

**Works in ANY situation:**
*"I'm curious - what's your current process for [notary scheduling/coordination/management]?"*

Then listen. They'll tell you exactly where it hurts.
