# Monaco

Monaco is Adrata's business intelligence platform that provides advanced prospecting capabilities, alternative data sources, and AI-powered research tools to help you discover and understand your target market.

## Overview

Monaco combines multiple data sources and AI capabilities to provide comprehensive business intelligence:

- **Prospect Discovery** - Find and research potential customers
- **Alternative Data** - Access unique data sources beyond traditional methods
- **AI Research** - Automated company and market research
- **Buyer Group Analysis** - Identify decision makers and influencers
- **Market Intelligence** - Industry insights and competitive analysis

## Getting Started

### 1. Access Monaco

1. Log in to your Adrata workspace
2. Navigate to **Monaco** from the main menu
3. You'll see the Monaco dashboard with search options

### 2. Search for Companies

1. Use the search bar to find companies by name, industry, or keywords
2. Apply filters to narrow down results
3. Review company profiles and select relevant prospects
4. Add companies to your pipeline or research queue

### 3. Research Companies

1. Click on a company to view detailed information
2. Review company profile, news, and key personnel
3. Use AI research tools for deeper analysis
4. Export findings or add to your CRM

### 4. Analyze Buyer Groups

1. Select a company for buyer group analysis
2. Review identified decision makers and influencers
3. Understand organizational structure and roles
4. Plan your outreach strategy

## Search Capabilities

### Basic Search

Search for companies using:

- **Company Name** - Exact or partial company names
- **Industry** - Industry categories and subcategories
- **Location** - Geographic regions and cities
- **Size** - Company size by employees or revenue
- **Keywords** - Any relevant keywords or phrases

### Advanced Filters

Refine your search with advanced filters:

- **Technology Stack** - Companies using specific technologies
- **Funding Status** - Recently funded or seeking investment
- **Growth Stage** - Startup, growth, or enterprise companies
- **Recent News** - Companies with recent news or events
- **Hiring Trends** - Companies actively hiring
