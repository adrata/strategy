Mental Health is the Retention Lever

Mental health is the #1 reason students drop out. WGU's 192,000 adult learners face unique stressors: work-life balance, family obligations, financial pressure, and isolation of online learning. Mantra's 70% "stayed enrolled" outcome2 directly addresses WGU's 62% retention rate6. WGU Labs has already piloted peer support with Flourish Labs7—they're actively seeking scaled mental health solutions.
