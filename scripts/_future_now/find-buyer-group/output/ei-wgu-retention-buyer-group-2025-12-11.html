E&I Retention Solution → WGU Product-Market Fit

Western Governors University serves 175,000+ students with a 100% online, competency-based model focused on adult learners. Student retention is critical to their mission and financial sustainability. With ~60:1 student-to-faculty ratio, WGU needs technology-enabled retention tools to identify at-risk students and enable proactive intervention. A ~$1M retention solution requires C-suite approval (CEO, Provost), a strong internal champion (SVP Academic Delivery), and buy-in from Finance and IT gatekeepers.
