# Retention Buyer Group Discovery - Comprehensive Audit

## Research Findings: Higher Education Retention Buyer Groups

### Key Roles Identified

**Decision Makers (Budget Authority):**
- Vice President for Student Affairs
- Vice President for Academic Affairs
- Provost / Vice Provost
- Chief Student Officer (CSO)
- Senior Vice President (Academic/Student Services)

**Champions (Operational Leaders):**
- Dean of Student Success
- Dean of Student Services
- Director of Student Success
- Director of Retention
- Director of Academic Advising
- Director of Student Engagement
- Director of First-Year Experience
- Assistant Provost for Student Success

**Stakeholders (Implementation Impact):**
- Director of Academic Support Services
- Director of Institutional Research
- Director of Analytics
- Manager of Retention
- Manager of Student Success
- Retention Specialist

**Blockers (Procurement/Legal/Finance):**
- Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
- Director of Procurement
- Legal Counsel
- IT Security (for data privacy concerns)

## Current System Audit

### ✅ Strengths

1. **Custom Filtering Configuration**
   - Well-structured primary/secondary department filtering
   - Good title matching for education-specific roles
   - Proper exclusion of irrelevant departments

2. **Smart Scoring**
   - Multi-dimensional scoring (seniority, department fit, influence)
   - Custom filtering integration
   - Deal size awareness

3. **AI Reasoning**
   - Profile relevance analysis
   - Role assignment reasoning
   - Buyer group validation

### ⚠️ Gaps Identified

1. **Missing Titles**
   - Director of Academic Advising
   - Director of First-Year Experience
   - Director of Academic Support Services
   - Retention Specialist
   - Assistant Provost for Student Success (specific)

2. **AI Prompts Lack Education Context**
   - Generic B2B prompts don't account for education hierarchies
   - No mention of Provost/Dean structures
   - Missing education-specific decision-making patterns

3. **Scoring Logic**
   - Doesn't account for Provost > VP > Dean hierarchy
   - Education-specific seniority scoring could be improved
   - Deal size thresholds may not match education purchasing patterns

4. **Role Assignment**
   - Generic role assignment doesn't understand education structures
   - Missing education-specific role patterns

## Recommendations

### 1. Expand Title Matching
Add missing titles to primary/secondary lists:
- Director of Academic Advising (primary)
- Director of First-Year Experience (primary)
- Director of Academic Support Services (primary)
- Retention Specialist (secondary)
- Assistant Provost for Student Success (primary)

### 2. Enhance AI Prompts
- Add higher education context to all prompts
- Include education hierarchy (Provost > VP > Dean > Director)
- Mention education-specific purchasing processes
- Add retention-specific decision-making patterns

### 3. Improve Scoring Logic
- Add education-specific seniority scoring
- Better recognition of Provost/Dean structures
- Account for education purchasing patterns ($500K-$1.4M deals)

### 4. Enhance Role Assignment
- Education-specific role patterns
- Better understanding of who approves retention solutions
- Account for shared governance in education
