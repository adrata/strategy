THE VIRAL EQUATION

K > 1.0 = Viral (each user brings more than one)
Target: 25% invite rate × 40% accept rate

K = invite rate × accept rate × users per invite
WHY VP SALES IS DIFFERENT: Average tenure 17-19 months. Only 43% hit quota. They share what helps them survive. Growth must feel like GAINING insight, not being marketed to.

Section 1: Viral Loops

Personal Referral Links
Every user gets unique URL. Show "X referrals to next reward" to accelerate invites (Goal Gradient).
Two-Sided Rewards
"Your colleague gets 20% off, you get Pipeline Score unlock." Exploits Reciprocity.
Invite-to-Unlock
Free tier gets 3 diagnostics. Invite 2 colleagues = unlock 5 more (Partial Unlock psychology).
Waitlist Jump
"Move from #847 to #12 by referring 3 friends." Scarcity + Endowed Progress + Goal Gradient.
Job Change Detection
Reps change jobs every 18-24 months. Detect LinkedIn changes: "Bring your Score to your new role!"

Section 2: Shareable Outputs

Pipeline Exposure Score Card
Beautiful visual with their score vs benchmark. "Powered by [Brand]" footer. Designed for LinkedIn.
Win Celebration Cards
Auto-generate "I closed a $X deal!" cards for LinkedIn. One-click share.
Weekly Stats Cards
"47 calls, 12 meetings, $120K pipeline added" — shareable like Spotify Wrapped.
Achievement Badges
"100 Deals Analyzed" — shareable badges. Pseudo-Set Framing—collect the full set.

Section 3: "Powered By" Everywhere
