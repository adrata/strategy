Part 4: Breaking False Beliefs

The Belief-Breaking Formula

Step 1: Name their false belief ("You probably think...")

Step 2: Validate it ("I thought the same thing...")

Step 3: Tell the Epiphany Bridge story ("Until I discovered...")

Step 4: Introduce the new belief ("Now I know that...")
