Category 1: Pipeline & Forecasting

The 72-Hour Slip Test
Identify which deals will slip before they do
LinkedIn Hook
"I can tell within 72 hours whether your 'committed' deal will slip. Not because I'm psychic. Because there are 3 signals that predict slippage with 87% accuracy."

THE 3 SIGNALS

  1. Champion Availability: Can you get a meeting in 72 hours? If NO → 73% slip rate
  2. Next Step Specificity: Is next step specific person + specific date? If VAGUE → 68% slip rate
  3. Decision Maker Engagement: Has economic buyer participated in 14 days? If NO → 81% slip rate

SCORING: 3/3 = Committed • 2/3 = At Risk • 1/3 = Will Slip • 0/3 = Already Dead

The Zombie Deal Detector
Clear dead weight from pipeline

5 ZOMBIE-KILLING QUESTIONS

  1. When was your last 2-way communication? → 21+ days = Zombie
  2. Has the deal stage changed in 30 days? → Same stage = Zombie
  3. Do you have the decision-maker's mobile? → No = Zombie
  4. Can you explain WHY they'd buy NOW? → Can't articulate = Zombie
  5. If you called your champion, would they answer? → Hesitation = Zombie
Pipeline Exposure Score
Single number showing pipeline health

RISK FACTORS (2+ = Exposed)

  • Single-threaded (only 1 contact)
  • No next step in 14 days
  • Champion silent 10+ days
  • No economic buyer engagement ever
  • Close date pushed more than once
  • Competitor actively engaged

BENCHMARKS: 0-20% = Healthy • 21-35% = Caution • 36-50% = Trouble • 51%+ = Emergency

Category 2: Deal Acceleration

The Champion Test
Know if you have a real champion or just a contact

5 CHAMPION QUESTIONS

  1. Access: Will they introduce you to decision-maker? Champion: Yes • Contact: "They're busy"
  2. Intel: Will they share real timeline? Champion: Truth • Contact: Party line
  3. Selling: Can they pitch you internally? Champion: Clear pitch • Contact: "I'll forward materials"
  4. Fighting: Will they handle pushback? Champion: "I'll handle it" • Contact: "Could be a problem"
  5. Reputation: Are they personally sponsoring? Champion: "My project" • Contact: "I'm supporting..."

SCORING: 5/5 = Real Champion • 3-4 = Potential • 1-2 = Contact • 0 = Nothing

PULL Qualification
Only pursue deals with real urgency

P.U.L.L. = Project + Urgency + Limitations + Leap

P - PROJECT: Is this an active project? "What's driving you to look now?"

U - URGENCY: Why solve NOW? "What happens if you don't solve by [date]?"

L - LIMITATIONS: Are alternatives inadequate? "What have you tried?"

L - LEAP: Can they buy? "What's the process to move forward?"
