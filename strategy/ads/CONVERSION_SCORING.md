## Scoring Guide

### Curiosity Gap (15 points)
- **10**: Headline creates irresistible need to know more
- **7-9**: Strong hook, clear value proposition
- **4-6**: Decent headline, but answer is obvious
- **1-3**: Generic, no curiosity created

### Dream Outcome Clarity (15 points)
- **10**: Visitor knows exact transformation in <3 seconds
- **7-9**: Clear benefit, takes a moment to process
- **4-6**: Benefit buried or unclear
- **1-3**: No clear outcome communicated

### Urgency/Scarcity (15 points)
- **10**: Genuine scarcity + countdown + limited spots visible
- **7-9**: Some urgency elements present
- **4-6**: Weak or generic urgency ("limited time")
- **1-3**: No urgency whatsoever

### CTA Power (15 points)
- **10**: First-person ("Get My..."), action verb, value-focused
- **7-9**: Action-oriented but generic ("Start Now")
- **4-6**: Passive ("Learn More", "Submit")
- **1-3**: Confusing or missing CTA

### Social Proof (10 points)
- **10**: Testimonials + logos + specific numbers
- **7-9**: Some social proof present
- **4-6**: Generic claims without specifics
- **1-3**: No social proof

### Visual Direction (10 points)
- **10**: Squiggles/arrows clearly guide eye to CTA
- **7-9**: Some visual hierarchy pointing to CTA
- **4-6**: CTA visible but not emphasized
- **1-3**: CTA hidden or hard to find

### Friction Removal (10 points)
- **10**: Crystal clear ask, feels easy ("15 min", "free", "no obligation")
- **7-9**: Low friction, some clarity
- **4-6**: Ask unclear or feels like commitment
- **1-3**: High friction, scary ask

### Emotional Trigger (10 points)
- **10**: Hits specific pain point that resonates deeply
- **7-9**: Good emotional copy, feels relevant
- **4-6**: Generic pain points
- **1-3**: No emotional connection

## Benchmark Targets

- **90+**: Elite, world-class conversion
- **80-89**: Excellent, minor optimizations needed
- **70-79**: Good, some gaps to address
- **60-69**: Average, significant room for improvement
- **<60**: Needs major overhaul
