How to Use Social Proof

When to Use

  • Opening conversations - establishes credibility
  • Validating their pain - "You're not alone"
  • Creating urgency - "Everyone's solving this"
  • Overcoming skepticism - hard numbers beat promises
  • Justifying investment - ROI proof points

Best Practices

  • Match testimonials to prospect's specific pain point
  • Use specific numbers and timeframes
  • Include role/title for credibility
  • Tell the before/after story
  • Keep stats current and verifiable
