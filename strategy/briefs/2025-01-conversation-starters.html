Cold Outreach (Email/LinkedIn)

Subject Lines That Get Opened

"Quick question about [Company Name]'s sales process"
"Saw you're hiring - scaling pains?"
"47 deals → 1 click"

Email Template #1: Problem-Aware

Hi [Name], Quick question - when a deal goes silent 30 days before quarter end, what's your backup plan? Most VPs of Sales we work with were spending 10+ hours/week chasing data. Now they spend 20 minutes. Worth a 15-minute conversation? [Your Name]

Email Template #2: Growth-Focused

Hi [Name], Noticed [Company Name] is expanding. Congrats! Quick question: How are you planning to handle 2x the volume without 2x the coordination headaches? Three companies in [Industry] solved this problem last quarter. Want to see how? [Your Name]

Referral Introductions

When Someone Introduces You

"[Referrer] mentioned you're dealing with pipeline visibility challenges. They said the same thing before we helped them increase forecast accuracy by 35%. Mind if I show you what we did?"

When You're Asking for a Referral

"Who else do you know in sales leadership who's frustrated with data silos? I'd love to help them the way we helped you."

Event/Networking Openers

At Industry Events

  • "What's the biggest operational challenge you're facing right now?"
  • "How many deals are you managing? How are you tracking engagement?"
  • "If you could automate one thing in your sales process, what would it be?"

The Follow-Up

"You mentioned [pain point] at the conference. I've been thinking about that - we just helped someone with the exact same issue. Got 10 minutes this week?"

Follow-Up Sequences

After Initial Contact (No Response)

Day 3:

"[Name] - following up on my note about pipeline intelligence. Bad timing? Or not a priority right now?"

Day 7:

"Last one, I promise! Quick question: If I could show you how to save 10 hours/week on reporting, would that be worth 15 minutes?"

After Demo (Moving to Close)

Day 1:

"Thanks for your time today! What was the most interesting part for you?"

Day 3:

"Been thinking about your comment on [pain point]. Here's how [similar client] solved that exact issue..."
