Content That Works

Themes that resonate with VP Sales:

Deal Slippage & Pipeline Risk

  • "3 signals a deal is about to stall that most AEs miss"
  • "Why your Q4 pipeline is lying to you"

Buying Committee Complexity

  • "Why your champion might be your biggest risk"
  • "The stakeholder you're not talking to (but should be)"

Contrarian Takes

  • "Unpopular opinion: Multi-threading is taught wrong"
  • "The worst sales advice I ever received (and followed)"

Posting Cadence

  • Minimum: 2-3 posts per week
  • Best times: Tuesday-Wednesday, 10 AM - 12 PM
  • Format priority: Video > Carousel > Text with image > Text only

DM Templates

Post Engagement
When they liked or commented on your post
Hey [First Name] – saw you liked my post on [topic]. Curious what resonated? Always looking to learn what's top of mind for sales leaders right now.
Comment Follow-Up
When they left a thoughtful comment
[First Name] – really appreciated your comment on [specific point]. That's exactly the perspective I was hoping to hear. Would love to continue the conversation if you're open to it.
Profile View
When they viewed your profile
Hey [First Name] – noticed you checked out my profile. I work with VPs of Sales at [industry] companies on [brief value prop]. Was there something specific that caught your eye?
Multi-Engagement → Meeting
When they've engaged multiple times
[First Name] – I've noticed you engaging with a few of my posts on [theme]. Seems like [problem] might be on your radar. We help VPs of Sales get visibility into buying committees before deals stall. Worth a 15-minute chat?
Follow-Up (No Response)
2-5 days after initial message
[First Name] – just circling back. I know VPs of Sales are slammed. If now's not right, no worries. But if [problem] is something you're wrestling with, happy to share how we've helped teams like yours.

Where Cold Calling Fits

Cold Calling Works Best as a Follow-Up Channel

Not as primary activity. A prospect who saw your ad → then your content → then gets a call? That's warm.

This Week

Actions

  • Post 2-3x from personal profile
  • 30 min/day engaging with VP of Sales content
  • Track who engages with your content
  • DM anyone who likes/comments within 24-48 hours

What Not to Do

  • Don't mass-DM cold. Generic templates don't work.
  • Don't pitch immediately. Build rapport first.
  • Don't give up after one touch. Multi-step sequences boost response 50%+.
  • Don't treat channels as separate. Coordinate content, DMs, and ads.
