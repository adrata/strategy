Tracking & Measurement

Calendar Intelligence

We have direct access to Dan's calendar data. Every metric below is tracked in real-time:

  • Fill Rate: % of available slots that are booked
  • Show Rate: % of booked meetings that actually happen
  • Conversion Rate: % of Offer calls that advance to Business Case
  • Velocity: Average days from Offer to Closed Won
  • Source Attribution: Which channels drive the highest-quality meetings

Success Metrics

We measure success by the destruction of empty calendar space:

  • Week 1 Target: 60% fill rate (51 meetings)
  • Week 2 Target: 75% fill rate (64 meetings)
  • Week 3 Target: 85% fill rate (72 meetings)
  • Week 4+ Target: 95% fill rate (81 meetings)
