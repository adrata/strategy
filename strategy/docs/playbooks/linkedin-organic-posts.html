LinkedIn Organic Posts

15 ready-to-use posts that convert VP Sales. Each mapped to buyer archetypes. Copy, customize, post.

The Content Strategy

Most LinkedIn content fails because it's either too salesy or too generic. The VP Sales you're targeting sees 47 posts before breakfast. They scroll past anything that smells like marketing.

What stops them? Content that names their specific fear. Content that proves you understand what keeps them up at 2 AM. Content that makes them think: "This person gets it."

The Content Mix

Balance these five types. Each serves a different purpose in warming up your audience.

Archetype Targeting

Each post below targets 1-2 of our core archetypes: The Anxious Elite (hitting number but pipeline slipping), The Worried Darling (looks successful but knows it's fragile), The Stressed Performer (losing despite working hard). Use their language. Name their enemy.

Posting Strategy

Timing

Best times: Tuesday-Wednesday, 10 AM - 12 PM. Avoid Monday morning (inbox overload) and Friday afternoon (weekend mindset).

Cadence

Minimum: 2-3 posts per week. Optimal: 4-5 posts per week. Consistency beats frequency—better to post 2x reliably than 5x inconsistently.

Format Priority

Video > Carousel > Text with image > Text only. But text-only contrarian posts often outperform everything else. Match format to content type.

The Golden Rule

Every post should pass this test: "Would a VP of Sales screenshot this and send it to their team?" If yes, post it. If no, keep refining.
