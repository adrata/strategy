The Closer's Challenge

You're measured on revenue, but half your week disappears into CRM updates, proposal drafting, meeting prep, and post-call admin. Every hour spent on paperwork is an hour not spent advancing deals.

AI agents handle the work around the work—so you can focus on what only you can do: building relationships and closing deals.

The Six Essential Agents for Account Executives

Pre-Call Intelligence Agent
Complete account briefings in 60 seconds

This agent prepares comprehensive briefings before every customer meeting. Company context, attendee backgrounds, relationship history, competitive landscape, and strategic opportunities—everything you need to walk in prepared and walk out with next steps.

Company Research: Recent news, earnings, leadership changes, strategic initiatives, and market position
Attendee Profiles: Background, priorities, communication style, and past interactions with your team
Deal Context: Where you are in the process, what's been discussed, what's outstanding
Recommended Questions: Discovery questions tailored to this specific meeting and attendee mix

Example: Pre-Call Brief for Globex QBR

Company: Q3 earnings beat expectations. New CRO started 45 days ago (from Oracle). Expanding APAC team by 40%.
Attendees: Sarah (champion, mentions "board pressure on efficiency"), Mike (new CRO, first meeting with him), Jennifer (procurement, historically asks about security).
Recommended: Ask Mike about his 90-day priorities. Prepare ROI summary—Sarah flagged she needs executive justification. Have SOC 2 report ready for Jennifer.

Deal Qualification Agent
MEDDIC/SPICED scoring from your conversations

This agent analyzes your discovery calls, emails, and notes to automatically score deals against your qualification methodology. It identifies gaps before your manager does—so you can address them in your next conversation.

Methodology Scoring: Automatic MEDDIC, SPICED, or BANT scoring based on actual conversations
Gap Identification: "No access to economic buyer" or "Compelling event undefined" flagged with specific remediation suggestions
Question Suggestions: Specific discovery questions to fill gaps in your next meeting
Win Probability: Data-driven close probability based on qualification strength and historical patterns

Proposal Generation Agent
Custom proposals in minutes, not hours

This agent drafts proposals and business cases using your discovery data. It pulls in the right case studies, customizes value propositions to their stated pains, and generates ROI calculations based on the metrics they shared.

Discovery-Driven Content: Pulls value propositions directly from what the customer said they care about
Case Study Matching: Selects the most relevant customer stories based on industry, size, and use case
ROI Calculation: Builds custom ROI models using the metrics discovered in your conversations
Competitive Positioning: Includes appropriate competitive differentiation if a competitor is involved

Generated Proposal: Acme Corp

Based on Discovery: "Your team spends 3 hours/day on manual data entry (Sarah, Oct 15). At 5 team members × $75/hr fully-loaded cost, that's $281K/year in wasted productivity. Our customers typically see 85% reduction in manual work, suggesting $239K in annual savings—a 191% ROI on your $125K investment."

Post-Call Summary Agent
Automatic meeting notes and CRM updates

This agent listens to your calls, generates structured notes, and updates your CRM automatically. No more spending 20 minutes after every call typing up what happened. The notes are there before your next meeting starts.

Structured Notes: Key discussion points, decisions made, objections raised, and action items—formatted consistently
CRM Auto-Update: Updates opportunity fields, next steps, and close date based on what was discussed
Follow-Up Draft: Generates a thank-you email with meeting recap and next steps, ready for your review
Stakeholder Updates: Adds new contacts to the opportunity and updates stakeholder information
Stakeholder Intelligence Agent
Multi-threading and champion development

This agent maps the buying committee, identifies influence patterns, and suggests multi-threading strategies. It tracks every stakeholder's engagement level and alerts you when someone goes cold.

Buying Committee Map: Decision makers, influencers, users, and potential blockers with engagement scores
Champion Health: Is your champion engaged? Have they gone quiet? Are they forwarding your emails internally?
Multi-Thread Suggestions: Who else should you be talking to? Who might block this deal?
Relationship Alerts: "No contact with CFO in 21 days" or "Legal hasn't responded to MSA redlines"
Deal Velocity Agent
Pipeline momentum and risk monitoring

This agent monitors the health of every deal in your pipeline. It detects stalls before they become losses, identifies deals that need attention, and suggests actions to get stuck opportunities moving again.

Stall Detection: Flags deals that haven't progressed according to stage-appropriate velocity
Risk Scoring: Combines qualification gaps, stakeholder engagement, and competitive signals into a risk score
Rescue Suggestions: Specific actions to revive stalled deals based on similar situations that worked
Commit Confidence: Should this deal be in commit? Based on data, not gut feel

How Agents Support the Deal Cycle

Discovery → Qualification

Pre-Call Intelligence ensures you walk into discovery prepared. Deal Qualification Agent scores what you learned against MEDDIC and identifies gaps for your next call.

Qualification → Solution Design

Post-Call Summary Agent captures everything discussed. Proposal Generation Agent starts building the business case with actual customer quotes and metrics.

Solution Design → Negotiation

Stakeholder Intelligence Agent ensures you're multi-threaded before the deal gets serious. Deal Velocity Agent tracks momentum and alerts you if engagement drops.

Negotiation → Close

Pre-Call Intelligence prepares you for exec meetings. Proposal Generation Agent updates ROI models for final business case. Post-Call Summary tracks every commitment made.
