The SDR's Challenge

You need to reach 100+ prospects per day. Each one should feel personalized. Research takes time. Templates feel fake. The math doesn't work—until now.

AI agents don't replace your hustle. They multiply it. They do the research in seconds so you can focus on what actually books meetings: the human touch.

The Five Essential Agents for SDRs

Prospect Research Agent
Deep research in seconds, not hours

This agent researches every prospect on your list before you reach out. Company context, recent news, tech stack, hiring patterns, leadership changes—everything you need to write an email that actually gets opened.

Company Intelligence: Recent funding, product launches, leadership changes, office openings, layoffs
Person Research: LinkedIn activity, recent posts, job history, common connections, interests
Tech Stack Detection: What tools they use (from job postings, website code, and public sources)
Trigger Events: Signals that suggest they might need your solution right now

Example: Research on Jennifer Martinez, VP Marketing at TechFlow

Company: TechFlow raised Series B ($35M) 3 weeks ago. Hiring 4 marketing roles. Just launched in EMEA.
Person: Jennifer joined 6 months ago from a competitor. Posted about "scaling demand gen" last week. Connected to 3 of your customers.
Trigger: Job posting for "Marketing Ops Manager" mentions "CRM integration challenges"—your exact use case.

Email Personalization Agent
Craft personalized first lines at scale

This agent writes personalized email openers based on real research—not fake personalization like "I saw you work at [Company]." It crafts first lines that prove you did your homework and gives recipients a reason to keep reading.

Research-Based Openers: First lines based on actual company news, prospect activity, or trigger events
Multi-Channel Personalization: Different hooks for email, LinkedIn, and cold calls
Pain Point Mapping: Connects research to the specific problems your product solves
A/B Testing: Generates multiple versions so you can test what resonates
Generated Email for Jennifer Martinez

Congrats on the Series B—and the EMEA expansion must be keeping your team busy.

I noticed you're hiring a Marketing Ops Manager and the job posting mentions "CRM integration challenges."

We helped Acme Corp solve that exact problem during their European expansion—they went from 3 hours of daily CRM cleanup to zero.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if we could help you too?

Call Prep Agent
30-second briefings before every dial

This agent prepares you for cold calls with a quick briefing on the prospect and company. You never go into a call blind—and you always have a relevant hook ready when they answer.

Quick Briefing: 3-4 bullet points on the person and company, optimized for a 30-second scan
Conversation Starters: 2-3 opening lines based on recent activity or news
Objection Prep: Common objections from this persona and suggested responses
Competitive Intel: If they use a competitor, key differentiators to mention
Account Mapping Agent
Find every relevant contact in target accounts

This agent identifies all the stakeholders you should be reaching in each account—not just the obvious titles, but the hidden influencers and rising stars who might champion your deal.

Stakeholder Identification: Decision makers, influencers, users, and potential blockers
Org Chart Building: Who reports to whom, recent promotions, new hires
Champion Signals: Who's most likely to respond based on role, tenure, and activity
Multi-Threading Strategy: Recommended sequence for reaching multiple contacts
Performance Coach Agent
Real-time feedback on your outreach

This agent analyzes your outreach performance and tells you what's working. Open rates, reply rates, meeting rates—by subject line, by persona, by time of day. It turns your activity data into actionable coaching.

Email Analytics: Which subject lines, openers, and CTAs are driving replies?
Call Analysis: Talk time, objection handling, appointment setting rate by approach
Persona Insights: Which titles are most responsive? Which industries?
Timing Optimization: Best days and times for outreach based on your actual data

The SDR's Daily Rhythm with Agents

8:00 AM

Morning Research Block

Prospect Research Agent has already analyzed today's 50 accounts overnight. You review the top 10 with the strongest trigger events and prioritize those for personalized outreach.

9:00 AM

Email Power Hour

Email Personalization Agent generates first lines for your priority list. You review, adjust tone where needed, and send. 25 truly personalized emails in 45 minutes instead of 3 hours.

10:30 AM

Cold Call Block

Call Prep Agent delivers a 30-second briefing before each dial. You're never caught off guard and always have a relevant hook. Connect rate: same. Conversation quality: dramatically higher.

2:00 PM

Account Expansion

Account Mapping Agent identified 3 additional stakeholders at accounts that went dark. You multi-thread into the CFO and a VP who just joined from a customer.

4:30 PM

Performance Review

Performance Coach Agent shows that emails mentioning "Series B" had 3x the reply rate this week. Tomorrow, you'll prioritize recently funded companies.
