Executive Summary

This document outlines our H1 2026 revenue strategy across three complementary channels: LinkedIn Enterprise Ads for high-value B2B deals, Instagram Prosumer Ads for affordable subscription revenue, and Enterprise Cold Calling for zero-cost pipeline generation.

The strategy is designed to be self-funding: enterprise cash flow funds prosumer acquisition, while cold calling provides a risk-free baseline. Combined, we project $18.1M revenue on $475K ad spend with $3.2M net cash by end of H1.

Organic Content Multiplier

LinkedIn research shows prospects exposed to both organic and paid content convert 61% higher than paid-only. Key tactics while ads ramp:

  • AEs post 2-3x/week from personal profiles (30 min/day)
  • Engage with ICP content (30 min/day) → warm DM (10-20% response vs 1-3% cold)
  • Boost top organic posts as Thought Leader Ads (carry social proof into paid)

Enterprise Cold Calling

Outbound calling to enterprise prospects. Zero ad spend—pure margin contribution. 1 person SDR/AE hybrid, 176 hours/month, 12 calls/hour with power dialer. 35-40% close rate on qualified meetings.

Strategic Context

Cold calling works best as a follow-up channel after digital touchpoints, not as a primary standalone activity. Reps using cold calls alongside email and LinkedIn see 28% higher conversion rates.

Key insight: 57% of C-level and VP-level buyers prefer phone contact—they hate bad cold calls, not all cold calls. Best times: 4-5 PM (71% more effective than mid-day), Wednesday (50% higher success than Mon/Tue).

Zero Ad Spend Advantage

Cold calling is free beyond labor. Every dollar of revenue is pure margin contribution. This channel de-risks the ad-dependent enterprise model.

Why This Works (Research-Backed)

The average cold-calling success rate in 2025 is 2.3% (Cognism)—almost half of 2024. But our model assumes warm-enhanced calling:

  • Prospects have seen LinkedIn ads or AE content first
  • We call during optimal windows (4-5 PM, Wednesday priority)
  • 76% of top performers "always" research before calling—we have Adrata data
  • Multi-touch coordination: ad → content → call = 28% higher conversion
