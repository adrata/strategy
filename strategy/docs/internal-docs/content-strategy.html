Great companies don't just sell products. They own ideas. This document outlines the foundational content pillars that will establish Adrata as the definitive voice in AI-powered sales intelligence.

The Acquired Framework: Timeless vs. Timely

The best content strategies balance two types of content: timeless pieces that compound in value over years, and timely pieces that capture moments and drive immediate engagement.

"The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now. The same is true for foundational content."

Timeless Content

Evergreen pieces that become reference material. These compound in search value and establish authority.

  • History of Sales Technology
  • The Greatest Salespeople of All Time
  • Sales Frameworks That Actually Work
  • The Psychology of Enterprise Deals
  • What Makes Revenue "Quality" Revenue

Timely Content

Pieces that capture the current moment. These drive social engagement and position us as thought leaders.

  • AI Agent Updates from Anthropic/OpenAI
  • Quarterly Sales Trend Reports
  • Conference Recaps and Analysis
  • New Tool/Platform Reviews
  • Breaking Industry News Takes

Content by Timing: The Calendar Strategy

Different moments in the year call for different content. Here's how to map content to the natural rhythms of sales organizations:

Q1 Start (Jan)

  • Annual Planning Guides
  • "State of AI in Sales" Report

Q2 Push (Apr-May)

Q3 Build (Jul-Sep)

  • Back-to-Business Momentum
  • Q4 Planning Starts Now
  • Budget Season Prep
  • Executive Engagement Guides

Q4 Close (Oct-Dec)

  • Year-End Deal Strategies
  • Budget Flush Opportunities
  • Forecast Accuracy Deep Dives

Monthly Moments

  • First Week of Month: Monthly pipeline review templates, "What Top Reps Do on Day 1"

  • Quarter End: End-of-quarter strategies, CFO conversations, procurement navigation

Content Formats: The Mix

Variety keeps audiences engaged. Here's the format palette:

  • Long-form Articles (2,000+ words) - SEO powerhouses, reference material
  • Quick Reads (500-800 words) - LinkedIn-friendly, shareable insights
  • Visual Frameworks - 2x2 matrices, flowcharts, comparison tables
  • Checklists & Templates - Downloadable, actionable, lead-gen worthy
  • Audio Versions - Every article gets a listen option (ElevenLabs voice)
  • Video Shorts - 60-second summaries for social
  • Data Reports - Original research, benchmark data, trend analysis
  • Interviews - Practitioner wisdom, real stories

The Compounding Effect

Here's the insight that separates good content strategies from great ones: timeless content compounds.

When someone searches "history of sales technology" in 2027, our piece should be the definitive result. When a VP of Sales asks their team "what are the best sales frameworks?" our frameworks piece should be the answer. When a founder researches AI agents for their sales team, our role-specific guides should dominate the conversation.

"Create content so good that people feel obligated to share it. Then create it so consistently that people feel obligated to subscribe."

What Success Looks Like

In 12 months, Adrata should be known for:

  1. The definitive history - When people want to understand sales technology evolution, they come to us
  2. The practical guides - When sales leaders want to implement AI, they use our role-specific playbooks

  4. The philosophical depth - When people want to think deeply about sales strategy, they reference our frameworks

This isn't about content volume. It's about content authority. Own the ideas, and you own the category.
