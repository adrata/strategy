Strategic Context

These matrices map the psychological state of a VP of Sales based on performance, trajectory, and organizational dynamics. The goal is to identify the absolute best audience segments to create ads that convert to meetings.

Key Success Metric: Hit their number in Q4 2025

Core Insight: PULL is everywhere. Buyers rip your product out of your hands when there's a project on their to-do list being prioritized right now, with urgency, limited options, and severe limitations in alternatives.

Ad Creative Strategy by Persona Tier

Tier 1: Anxious Winners (Hit + Slipping) — FEAR OF LOSING STATUS
  • Emotional trigger: Pride protection, fear of being exposed
  • Tone: Peer-to-peer, "I see what you see"
  • CTA: "See what's working for VPs who refuse to slip"
  • Proof point: Case studies of sustained performance
Tier 2: Hopeful Rebuilders (Missed + Recovering) — HUNGER FOR REDEMPTION
  • Emotional trigger: Second chance narrative, proving doubters wrong
  • Tone: Empowering, "Your story isn't over"
  • CTA: "Get the Q1 comeback playbook"
  • Proof point: Turnaround stories
Tier 3: Under-Resourced Fighters — SCRAPPY SURVIVAL
  • Emotional trigger: Doing more with less, self-reliance
  • Tone: Tactical, ROI-focused
  • CTA: "Pipeline tactics that don't require budget"
  • Proof point: Specific tactics, templates, frameworks
