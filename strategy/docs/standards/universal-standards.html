Universal Standards

Core standards for all Adrata content. Upload this with any task as context for AI or human reference.

Step 0: Always Start Here

Which archetype(s) is this targeting? Every piece of content must target 1-2 specific archetypes. Generic content converts nobody. Reference the Archetypes Persona document.

Voice Principles

Our voice is modeled on Q4: Chapter 1. Core traits:

  • Visceral: Emotions land physically. "The thought lands in my chest like a stone."
  • Specific: Exact numbers, names, times. "$18.4 million. 88 days."
  • First-Person: Reader IS the VP of Sales. They see themselves in every scene.
  • Show, Don't Tell: Reveal psychology through actions and scenes.
  • Questions that Pull: End with curiosity-creating questions.

Language by Archetype

Innovators (Anxious Elite, Frustrated Fighter, Rebuilding Leader):

Use: "AI Agents", "Autonomous Intelligence", "Buying Committee Mapping"

Early Adopters (Worried Darling, Hungry Underdog, Stretched Striver):

Use: "AI Sales Intelligence", "Predictive Deal Health", "Force Multiplier"

Early Majority (Stressed Performer, Recovering Performer):

Use: "Buyer Intelligence", "Revenue Intelligence", "Pipeline Analytics"

Late Majority (Scrambling Politician, Sweating Target):

Use: "CRM Intelligence", "Enterprise Proven", "Quick Wins"

Formatting Standards

  • Font: MiSans (always)
  • Accent color: #e65100 (orange)
  • Default theme: Dark mode
  • Max width: 800-900px
  • No emojis in strategy docs
  • Use Lucide icons if icons needed

Pre-Publish Checklist

  • Which 1-2 archetypes does this target?
  • Does the hook stop a stressed VP at 6 AM?
  • Does it name their enemy (core fear)?
  • Is there something they literally can't do themselves?
  • Does it feel like gaining insight, not being sold to?
  • Are all numbers specific, not vague?
  • Would they forward this to a peer?
