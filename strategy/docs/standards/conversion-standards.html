Page Structure

Above the Fold (0-5 seconds)

  • Headline: Names their enemy or promises the outcome they want
  • Subhead: Specificity—who this is for, what they get
  • Visual: Show the outcome (not the product)
  • CTA: Low-commitment first step

Social Proof Zone

  • Logos (if B2B enterprise)
  • Testimonial with photo, name, title, company
  • Specific result numbers ("37% increase in close rate")

The Offer

  • What they get (be specific)
  • What it costs (if free, say free)
  • How long it takes
  • What they have to do (minimal)

Form Design

  • Minimum fields: Name, Email, Company
  • Add fields only if they improve sales qualification significantly
  • Never ask for phone on first touch (unless high-intent)
  • Use dropdown for company size / role if needed for routing
  • Button text: Specific > Generic ("Get My 5 Account Maps" not "Submit")
