Psychological Triggers That Convert

  • FOMO (Fear of Missing Out): Can increase opt-ins by 38.25% when used ethically
  • Peer Comparison: 70%+ of executives use benchmarking data for decisions
  • Personalization: 86% of B2B buyers expect personalized experiences
  • Instant Value: Speed-to-lead directly correlates with conversion (66.7% with instant booking vs 30% without)
  • Social Proof: Case studies influence 84% of C-suite decisions
  • Risk Mitigation: Strongest driver for enterprise B2B purchases
  • Reciprocity: Give value first, then ask - 85% show rate when value pre-delivered
  • Loss Aversion: Fear of loss 2x stronger than desire for gain
  • Endowment Effect: People value what they've invested time in

Category 1: Diagnostic & Assessment Offers

Psychology: Self-discovery, validation of suspicions, personalized insights. Highest conversion rates.

Offer 1
Scorecard / Maturity Assessment
What it is: Interactive quiz scoring them across multiple categories (5-15 questions)
Psychology: Self-assessment + peer comparison. Shows what they're doing well AND what needs help.
Conversion: 30-50% (vs 3-10% for static PDFs)
Example names: "Sales Readiness Scorecard", "Pipeline Health Check", "Revenue Maturity Assessment"
Best for archetypes: ALL (universal appeal)
Offer 2
Gap Analysis
What it is: Shows the gap between current state and desired state
Psychology: Creates urgency by quantifying the problem. "Here's exactly how far behind you are."
Example names: "Sales Gap Analysis", "Performance Gap Report", "Speed Gap Benchmark"
Best for archetypes: Panicking Star, Desperate Hitter, Sweating Target (urgency-driven)
Offer 3
Pipeline Exposure Audit
What it is: Analysis of deals with hidden blockers, unmet stakeholders, risk signals
Psychology: Validates gut instinct. "The spreadsheet says fine. Your gut says no. Trust your gut."
Example names: "Pipeline Risk Assessment", "Deal Exposure Audit", "Committed Deal Diagnostic"
Best for archetypes: Anxious Elite, Stressed Performer, Worried Darling (suspicious)
Offer 4
Hidden Stakeholder Map
What it is: Visual org chart showing decision-makers your reps haven't reached
Psychology: Reveals blind spots. Makes invisible risks visible.
Example names: "Buying Committee Map", "Decision-Maker Blind Spot Analysis", "Stakeholder Gap Report"
Best for archetypes: Worried Darling, Panicking Star (need quick wins)
Offer 5
Marketing-Sales Alignment Score
What it is: 2-minute assessment diagnosing friction between marketing and sales
Psychology: Validates frustration. Provides autonomy. "Marketing isn't coming to save you."
Best for archetypes: Frustrated Fighter, Rebuilding Leader
Offer 6
Tool Sprawl Audit
What it is: Visual matrix showing tool overlap, conflict, redundancy
Psychology: Efficiency obsession. Waste elimination. "47 tools. None tell you who buys."
Best for archetypes: Stretched Striver, Hungry Underdog (resource-constrained)
Offer 7
Speed-to-Buyer Benchmark
What it is: Comparison of how fast they reach economic buyers vs. competitors
Psychology: Competitive anxiety. Peer comparison. FOMO.
Best for archetypes: Sweating Target, Panicking Star (time pressure)
Offer 8
Sales Process Diagnostic
What it is: Root cause analysis using Gap Selling methodology
Psychology: Problem → Impact → Root Cause framework. Deals with complete diagnostic coverage have 61% win rate vs 17%.
Best for archetypes: Rebuilding Leader, Desperate Hitter (need proven path)
